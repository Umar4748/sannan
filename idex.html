Company Overview

3A Sanai Health was founded in 2000 and came to life with an aspiration to combat respiratory illnesses and help people live healthier and longer. We feared the exponential rise in morbidity and mortality caused by the lack of reliable and high- quality respiratory care devices easily accessible by people of all social statuses. And it is how we originated as a triple-tiered love model, emphasizing love for Nature, Society, and Life.
